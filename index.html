Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Nationalism

Emphasizing unity and the identity of the Bangladeshi people, transcending ethnic, linguistic, or cultural differences, and promoting the spirit of independence and sovereignty.

Democracy

Ensuring a government by the people, for the people, with free and fair elections, safeguarding fundamental rights, and upholding accountability and rule of law.

Socialism

Representing the commitment to social justice and equitable distribution of resources to reduce poverty, inequality, and exploitation, aiming to create a society free from economic oppression.

Secularism

Guaranteeing freedom of religion, ensuring equal treatment of all religions, and promoting harmony and coexistence among diverse religious communities, while preventing the use of religion for political purposes.

The Vision Of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"My vision for the future? Two things: to make credit a human right so that each individual human being will have the opportunity to take loans and implement his or her ideas so that self-exploration becomes possible. And second: that it will lead to a world where nobody has to suffer from poverty - a world completely free from poverty."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
